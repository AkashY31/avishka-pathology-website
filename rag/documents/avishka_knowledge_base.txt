PREPARATION INSTRUCTIONS
-------------------------
- CBC: No fasting required
- Blood Sugar Fasting: Fast for 8–12 hours before the test
- Blood Sugar PP: Have the test 2 hours after a meal
- Lipid Profile: Fast for 12 hours before the test
- Thyroid Profile: No fasting required
- LFT: Fast for 12 hours before the test
- KFT: No fasting required
- Urine Test: Collect early morning mid-stream urine sample
- Vitamin D / B12: No fasting required
- HbA1c: No fasting required
- ECG: Wear comfortable, loose clothing
